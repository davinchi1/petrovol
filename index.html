PETROVOL


PETROVOL aims to enable equitable value distribution in the auto fuel industry for all participants.


For the first time in the petroleum industry, the consumer is being targeted as the ultimate beneficiary in the advancement of the industry.


PETROVOL is enabling the extension of true value in the auto fuel industry, right down to the everyday consumers at the end of the value chain. This is achieved by empowering the gasoline retailers with the ability to truly reflect payments based on actual value received from producers/distributors.


We are empowering retailers and ultimately the consumers with true value-for-money.


Our vision is to effect positive change to one of the last frontiers in the quest for universal measurement and standards, for the betterment of the world, one retailer at a time.
